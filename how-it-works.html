how it works

automation meets oversight

at jack does, we believe the future of accounting isn't about replacing people with technology — it's about using technology to free people up for the work that actually matters.

what jack does

the AI that handles the routine

  • processes and categorizes uploaded documents
  • proposes journal entries and bookkeeping transactions
  • answers your financial questions 24/7
  • generates real-time reports and insights
  • automates the repetitive, low-value-add tasks

what joel does

the professional who ensures quality

  • reviews every journal entry before it posts
  • reviews all client deliverables personally
  • handles complex accounting judgments and strategy
  • interacts directly with clients when needed
  • brings 15+ years of senior finance experience

the process

1

you upload

upload your invoices, receipts, bank statements, or any accounting documents through the client portal.

2

jack analyzes

jack processes the documents, extracts the relevant data, and proposes the appropriate accounting entries.

3

joel reviews

joel reviews jack's work, makes any necessary adjustments, and approves the entries before they post to your books.

4

you relax

your books are up to date, your financials are accurate, and you can check in with jack anytime for real-time insights.

the bottom line? you get the speed and efficiency of automation with the confidence that a real, experienced professional is reviewing everything before it reaches you.
