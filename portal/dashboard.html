important: CRA compliance

the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) expects to see invoice or receipt back-up for business expenses. if you do not have a supporting document, the CRA will generally disallow the expense upon review, which could also trigger further audit issues.

we highly recommend that you save all receipts and invoices.

physical document fee

processing physical documents requires additional handling time. there is an additional fee of $1.00 per physical document that will be added to your invoice.

once confirmed, we'll provide mailing instructions so you can send your physical receipts and invoices to us.
