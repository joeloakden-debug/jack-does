joel oakden

founder & owner

with over 15 years of experience in finance and accounting, joel has held senior leadership roles across a range of industries, including Chief Accounting Officer, Senior Vice President of Finance, and Group Controller.

throughout his career, joel saw the same problem everywhere: accounting firms relying on outdated, manual processes that wasted time, inflated costs, and left clients in the dark about their own financials.

he created jack does to solve that.

the problem

legacy accounting firms charge clients for hours spent on repetitive manual tasks that modern technology solved years ago. the result? slow turnaround times, high fees, and clients who have no real-time visibility into their business.

the solution

jack does leverages current technology — AI and automation — to streamline the accounting work, eliminate manual overhead, and give clients what they've always deserved: real-time insights into the financials of their business, at a fraction of the traditional cost.

the promise

technology does the heavy lifting, but there's always a real person behind the scenes. every client deliverable is reviewed by joel personally before it reaches you. you get the efficiency of automation with the oversight of an experienced professional.
